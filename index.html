About Us

CTN Policy is a Kenyan forward-thinking policy and research institute dedicated to reshaping public discourse on Law, Governance, Social Welfare, and the Economy. We provide insights, research, and policy recommendations to support informed decision-making.

Our Work

We conduct research, publish reports, and engage with stakeholders to influence policies in Kenya. Our focus areas include governance, law reform, social welfare programs, and economic development.
